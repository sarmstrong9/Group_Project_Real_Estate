Conclusions

The California Housing market is at an all time high. We are interested in looking into what might be causing such high prices and if we could figure out a way to predict how the market might move in the future.

In order to set ourselves up to best look in to this we decided to look at Redfin. Redfin is a website that contains current and past data for many housing metrics including but not limited to: buying, selling, and renting. Through our research we found a kaggle dataset that contains information reguarding US state to state migration. This provided us with data from the US Census that details state to state migration as well as population data from 2010 to 2019.
