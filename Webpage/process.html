Choosing and Cleaning Datasets

The RedFin dataset was 28,975 rows and 58 columns. Although this was a lot of good information, we had to decide what data would be the most important to our analysis. Using pandas, we cleaned and manipulated the data to only include information relating to our analysis. We settled on only including states, selling dates, property type, median sale price, and homes sold. To further narrow our results, we chose to primarily focus on California and Texas. This information was then separated into two dataframes for each respective state.
