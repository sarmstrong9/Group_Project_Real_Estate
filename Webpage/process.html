Choosing and Cleaning Datasets

Redfin

The RedFin dataset was 28,975 rows and 58 columns, which was a lot of good information but we had to decide what data would be the most important to our analysis.Using pandas, we cleaned and manipulated the data to only include information relating directly to our analysis. We settled on only including states, year, property type, median sale price, and homes sold. This allowed us to reduce the amount of data we had to process and also get a more focused result.

Kaggle Data

Kaggle

The Kaggle dataset we used is a user uploaded dataset that shows US Census data pertaining to migration. The dataset is easily readable and shows migration data from 2010 to 2019. The information is broken down by state and displays the migration count from all 50 states and total population.
